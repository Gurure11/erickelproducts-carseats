About Us

10 Years of Excellence

Erickel Car Seats & Covers has proudly served South Africa for over a decade, specializing in high-quality seat covers for Cars, Buses, and Trucks. We customise any model and add personalised logos for businesses, taxis, fleets, and private vehicle owners.

Our factory in Three Rivers, Vereeniging produces durable, stylish and superior-fitting covers. We use premium materials designed for comfort, protection and long-lasting performance.
